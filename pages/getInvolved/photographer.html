Become Our Official Photographer

Snap, snap, snap! Do you have a love for nature as well as photography? You’ve come to the right place. Manly Volunteer Penguin Wardens is always on the lookout for someone like you. Join our photography team and help showcase our penguins through the lens. Requirements:

  • Photographers must have their own Digital SLR Camera
  • Minimum age requirement is 16 years of age
If the above requirements sounds like you then please contact us at mec@manly.nsw.gov.au or come down and visit us at the Manly Environment Centre. Grab your camera and your boots and let the penguin journey begin. You never know you might even get the rare chance of seeing the chicks head out to sea for the first time.
